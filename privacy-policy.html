Privacy Policy

Privacy Overview

Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit or shop on our website. By using our services, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping details when you place an order. We also collect payment-related information for secure transaction processing. Additionally, non-personal data may be collected to improve website performance.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process orders, manage deliveries, and provide customer support. We may also use your data to communicate updates, offers, or important service information. All data usage is handled responsibly and only for business purposes.

Payment Security

All payments are processed through secure and trusted payment gateways. We do not store your card or payment details on our servers. Advanced security measures are used to protect your financial information. Your payment safety is a top priority.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information to third parties. Your data may only be shared with trusted service providers for order fulfillment and delivery purposes. These partners are required to keep your information confidential. Data sharing is limited and controlled.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and improve functionality. Cookies help us understand user behavior and optimize website performance. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. Some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information. If you wish to make changes or have concerns about your data, please contact us. We respect your privacy choices and aim to respond promptly. Your trust matters to us.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at info.mzvogue@gmail.com. Our team is available to assist with privacy-related concerns. We aim to respond within 24 hours.
