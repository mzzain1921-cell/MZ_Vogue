Shipping Information

Shipping Overview

We are committed to delivering your orders in a safe and timely manner. Every order is carefully packed to ensure product quality during transit. Our shipping process is designed to provide a smooth and reliable delivery experience. Customer satisfaction remains our top priority.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed on the next working day. During high-volume periods, processing times may slightly increase. We always strive to dispatch orders as quickly as possible.

Shipping Time

Once your order has been shipped, delivery usually takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. Shipping times may vary due to weather conditions or courier delays. Please note that estimated delivery times are not guaranteed. Tracking updates will keep you informed throughout the journey.

Order Tracking

After your order is dispatched, you will receive an email containing your tracking information. This allows you to monitor your shipment in real time. Tracking updates are provided by our delivery partners. If you face any issues, our support team is available to assist.

Delayed or Lost Orders

While we work closely with reliable carriers, delays may occasionally occur beyond our control. If your order has not arrived within the expected timeframe, please contact us for assistance. Our team will investigate the issue and provide updates. Customer support is always here to help.

Address Changes

Address modifications can only be requested before the order has been shipped. Once an order is dispatched, we are unable to make changes to the delivery address. Please review your shipping details carefully at checkout. This helps avoid unnecessary delays.

Damaged Packages

If your package arrives damaged, contact us within 48 hours of delivery. Please provide clear photos of the package and product for review. Our team will assess the issue and arrange a suitable resolution. Your satisfaction is important to us.

Contact for Shipping Queries

For any shipping-related questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to our support team. You can contact us via email at info.mzvogue@gmail.com . We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. Our team is always happy to assist you.
