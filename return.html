Return & Exchange

Return & Exchange Overview

We want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your order, we offer a simple and transparent return and exchange process. Our policy is designed to ensure fairness and convenience for our customers. Please review the details below before requesting a return or exchange.

Eligibility for Returns

Items are eligible for return or exchange within 7 days of delivery. Products must be unused, unwashed, and in their original condition with tags attached. Items showing signs of wear or damage may not be accepted. We reserve the right to decline returns that do not meet these requirements.

How to Request a Return or Exchange

To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our support team with your order number and reason for the request. Our team will guide you through the process step by step. Requests made without prior approval may not be processed. We recommend reaching out as soon as possible after delivery.

Exchange Process

Exchanges are subject to product availability at the time of request. Once the returned item is received and inspected, we will process the exchange accordingly. If the requested item or size is unavailable, an alternative or store credit may be offered. Exchange processing times may vary.

Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item received was defective or incorrect. We recommend using a trackable shipping service to ensure safe delivery. Shipping charges are non-refundable. We are not responsible for items lost during return shipping.

Refunds

Once your returned item is received and inspected, refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at checkout. Processing times may vary depending on your payment provider. You will receive confirmation once the refund is completed.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. Provide clear photos of the issue to help us resolve it quickly. After verification, we will arrange a replacement or refund. Customer satisfaction is our priority.

Contact Support

For any questions related to returns or exchanges, feel free to contact our support team via email at info.mzvogue@gmail.com. Our team aims to respond within 24 hours. We’re here to make your shopping experience smooth and stress-free.
